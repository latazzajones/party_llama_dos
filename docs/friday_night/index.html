R. House

R. House is a food hall in the Remington neighborhood of Baltimore. They feature vendors serving food from Venezuela to Korea and everywhere in between.

Time

We've reserved the den starting at 7:00 PM. The den is the area with couches in the corner furthest away from all the food.

This event is OPTIONAL

If you're around and wanna be social, feel free to swing by. If you're running late or won't be in town until Saturday, don't worry about it. Friday night is a totally-optional-stress-free event.

Address

R. House is located at 301 W 29th St, Baltimore, MD 21211. It's about 3.5 miles from our wedding hotels.

Parking

Click here to see a map of all the parking options. P2 is small and fills up pretty quickly. P1 is the police lot catty-corner from R. House. There is also street parking.
