The Wedding

Should I wear two outfits?

That's totally up to you! The most important thing is that you're comfortable and feel like a million bucks. That being said, it might be nice to have a fresh shirt if the weather is hot.

Will there be food?

Yes! We’ll have tons of food at the picnic and both savory and sweet food at the cake reception.

How do we get from the picnic to the cake reception?

We will be providing busses to take people back to the Hyatt Place Hotel. Once you get there, there will be a 2 hour break. The cake reception is walking distance from the Hyatt Place Hotel.
