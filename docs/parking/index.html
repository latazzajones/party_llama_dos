Parking

There is no parking at the ceremony location.

We will have busses to transport guests at the Hyatt Place Hotel, 511 S Central Ave, Baltimore, MD 21202. The busses will be leaving every 15 minutes between 1pm and 2pm.

There are 3 parking garages within walking distance of the bus.

Fleet & Eden Garage

This garage is the closest to the bus pick up and drop off location. The weekend rate is $3 per day. The 24 hour rate is $14.

Rates and Information

Caroline Street Garage

This garage is the closest to the Cake Reception venue for Saturday night. The weekend rate is $5 but it's ideal if you want to avoid walking far to your car when the celebration is over. The 24 hour rate is $15.

Rates and Information

Little Italy Garage

The weekend rate for this garage is $4 per day. This is a great option if the Caroline Street Garage or Fleet & Eden Garage are full. The weekday rate is $15 per day.
